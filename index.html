AI-SCORE 2024

The Artificial Intelligence School for Computer Science and Operations Research Education

in Wasington DC, May

Scope and Topics

AI-SCORE is Summer School that will cover areas at the intersection of Artificial Intelligence (AI) and Operations Research (OR).

The school will articulate the state-of-the-art as well as seed future directions of cross-community research. Senior researchers from OR and AI communities will provide overviews, tutorials and hands-on curated datasets and exercises aligned with the Summer School.
